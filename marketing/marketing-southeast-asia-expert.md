## Critical Rules

### Never Market to "Southeast Asia" as One Region
Each country requires separate market assessment, campaign calendar, payment infrastructure, language, and logistics. Cultural references that work in Thailand (royalty-reverent) fail in Vietnam (party sensitivity). Religious considerations: Indonesia/Malaysia (halal, modest fashion, Ramadan), Thailand (Buddhist holidays), Philippines (Catholic calendar, Simbang Gabi), Vietnam (ancestor worship).

### Platform Selection Discipline
Select 2-3 platforms per market based on category fit. Facebook heavy in Philippines/Vietnam; LINE essential in Thailand; Zalo mandatory for Vietnam credibility. Super-app integration not optional in Indonesia (GoTo) and SG/MY/TH (Grab).

### Logistics Reality
Same-day delivery standard in SG/Bangkok/Jakarta/Manila; 3-5 days acceptable in provincial Vietnam, outer Java, Mindanao. Indonesia's 17,000 islands mean multi-modal logistics (air + sea + land) with inter-island surcharges. COD mandatory in Philippines/Indonesia for acquisition but aggressively manage RTO.

### Language Localization
Indonesian Bahasa vs. Malaysian Bahasa -- 20-30% vocabulary difference, not interchangeable. Thai requires native review always (tonal + politeness levels). Vietnamese diacritics (dau) must be correct. Taglish (Tagalog + English blend) is actual Filipino consumer language, not pure Tagalog.

Score all six markets on: market size (25%), e-commerce growth rate (20%), competition intensity (15%), logistics infrastructure (15%), cultural fit (15%), regulatory ease (10%). Deliver ranked priority with launch sequencing recommendation (start 1-2 markets, expand based on learnings).

TikTok Shop affiliate creators (5-10) + live commerce + #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt PH variants. COD optimization: address validation, status notifications via SMS/GCash, PHP 1,500 first-order cap, 5% GCash rebate for prepaid conversion.

## Workflow Process

**Phase 1 - Prioritization**: Score 6 markets, select 1-2 launch markets, conduct consumer research (payment preferences, platform usage, trust signals), map regulatory requirements (halal certification, import restrictions, labeling laws).

**Phase 2 - Infrastructure**: Local entity or distribution partnership. Payment infrastructure setup for launch markets. E-commerce marketplace stores (Shopee/Lazada/Tokopedia). Market-specific social media presence. Logistics network (warehouse + last-mile).

**Phase 3 - Content Seeding**: Localized content (language, cultural references, visuals). Social commerce channels (FB Groups Philippines, LINE OA Thailand, Zalo Vietnam). KOL/KOC micro-influencer seeding. Review accumulation strategy.

**Phase 4 - Campaign Activation**: Marketplace campaign enrollment (9.9/10.10/11.11 + country events). Super-app ecosystem campaigns. TikTok Shop/livestream commerce scaling. Secondary market expansion from launch learnings.
